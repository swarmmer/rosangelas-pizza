Our History

Rosangela's Pizza was founded in 1955 by an Italian immigrant with a passion for traditional, high-quality food. From the very beginning, our goal was simple: to be your favorite neighborhood pizzeria.

We're a throwback to the wonderful, old-time pizzerias you grew up with. From our vintage neon sign out front to the wood-paneled dining room, we've stayed true to our roots. For over 65 years, we've served our famous thin-crust pizza to our friends and neighbors in Evergreen Park.

We're still family-owned, still using the same classic recipes, and still proud to be a cornerstone of the southside community. It's the kind of place where our staff chats with regulars and every customer is treated like part of the family. We're proud of our history and look forward to serving you for many years to come.
