Welcome to the Family

For over 60 years, Rosangela's has been a cornerstone of the Evergreen Park community. We're not just a pizza place; we're a family. Every pie is made with the same traditional recipes and quality ingredients that have made us a Southside favorite since 1955.

From our famous thin-crust pizza to our classic Italian-American dinners, we invite you to stop by, say hello, and taste the tradition. We're proud to serve you.
